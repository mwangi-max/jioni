Service Delivery Unit - Kiambu County

The Service Delivery Unit is an entity domiciled in the Excecutive Office of the Governor, Kiambu County and which was established to coordinate, monitor and evaluate projects in the various County departments and as such, give recommendations to the County Government of Kiambu for the timely fulfilment of H.E. the Governor development priorities.

The overarching agendas of SDU as it commonly referred to includes but not limited to:

  • Enhance coordination of projects/programs/inititaives
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Data collection, analzying, and provision of a reporting framework
  • Tracking and reporting on prioritized projects
  • Problem solving and practical interventions to unlock delays
  • Amplification on progress and achievements

It is headed by Chief Officer, Ms. Julie Waweru
